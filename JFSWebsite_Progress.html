The Origin of the Channel

JustFaithSilly used to have videos of clay videos back in 2013. I decided to private them because I think they weren't a good fit to the channel and I think they aren't funny anymore. The channel went on hiatus for 5 years with no other videos posted with an exception of 2017, which has one video. In 2018, the channel made a comeback, surprising viewers with fan-made animations that spike up with love, care and has a lot of views and fans.

  • 2007 - My dad wanted me to say my name, and I said, "It's just Faith, silly". That sentence is the origin of where JustFaithSilly came from.
  • 2013 - My YouTube was created in June 28, 2013. The first video was uploaded the day after. After a few videos were uploaded, the channel went on hiatus for a long time.
  • 2017 - In November, the YouTube channel decided to post one more video before possibly going on another hiatus or planning for another new level for the channel
  • 2018 - Starting from June, the YouTube channel made a surprise return by posting episodes of a fan-made series based on her favorite shows and video games. Those videos started off slow for a return, but began to grow to be an amazing comeback for the fans.
  • 2019 - I started an Instagram channel based on my YouTube Channel. I posted artwork and screenshots from my videos.
  • 2020 - The YouTube channel gained 10,000 subscribers in March during the COVID pandemic.
  • 2022 - My Twitter/X account is created. I mostly use it for liking posts and commenting, while I slightly post on Twitter, but not that much.
  • 2023 - My TikTok account is created. It hasn't been active since because I've been thinking of my first video to post
