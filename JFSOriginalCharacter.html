JustFaithSilly's Original Character

Penny

Penny is a pink ballpoint pen that has been debuted in 'Pen's Baby Sister' on March 30th 2019

Penny is one of JustFaithSilly's first characters ever created and is one of the most beloved and well-known characters ever created.

Ever since Penny's debut, the video gained more than one million views and became one of JustFaithSilly's most popular characters ever created.

What makes Penny popular?

When there's an episode focusing on Penny, she's the main character of the episode

Penny has been making several appearances when she's a minor character or a cameo

Penny appeared on not only YouTube, but also on Instagram, Facebook, X, and TikTok
